FAQ

Common questions.

How much can PDF-X reduce my file size?

Results vary by document, but most files see reductions between 40% and 80% depending on the quality preset you choose.

Will compression reduce the visual quality of my PDF?

PDF-X offers quality presets so you control the balance between file size and visual fidelity — choose High Quality for minimal visible difference.

Are my files uploaded to the cloud?

No. All compression happens entirely on your iPhone. Your documents never leave your device.

Can I compress scanned PDFs?

Yes. Scanned documents and image-heavy PDFs can both be compressed, often yielding the largest size savings.

Is PDF-X free to use?

Yes. PDF-X has a free experience with optional low-cost upgrade plans for power users who need unlimited compression.
