FAQ

Will my formatting be preserved after conversion?

PDF-X preserves text layout, headings, and basic formatting for clean, editable Word output — ready to use without heavy reformatting.

Can I convert scanned PDFs to Word?

Yes, PDF-X can convert both digital and scanned PDFs to Word format, extracting readable text from your scanned pages.

Does it work with large or multi-page PDFs?

Yes. Multi-page documents convert cleanly with page structure and text flow maintained throughout the document.

Which Word format does PDF-X export?

PDF-X exports standard .docx format, compatible with Microsoft Word, Google Docs, Apple Pages, and most word processors.
